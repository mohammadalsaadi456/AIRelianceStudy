Research Timeline

Our 8-month journey from idea to results

September 2025

Formed team, selected research topic, and wrote project proposal

October 2025

Literature Review

Studied existing research on AI ethics, education, and mental health

November 2025

Ethics Approval

Received ethics clearance from Carleton University to work with human participants

December 2025

Student Interviews

Interviewed 8 Carleton students about their AI use and opinions

January 2026

Framework Creation

Developed 5 principles for responsible AI use based on interview findings

February 2026

Conducted controlled experiment with 10 students to test our framework

March 2026

Analyzed experiment results and prepared findings

April 2026
