AI Reliance Study

A Student Led Framework for Responsible AI Use in Schoolwork

Exploring the effects of AI in education and developing practical guidelines for responsible implementation through research, interviews, and experiments.

Explore Framework View Findings

Project Overview

Understanding AI reliance through comprehensive research

Goal

Develop a practical framework for responsible AI use in academic settings, supported by moral and academic ethics.

Research

Analyze AI usage patterns, ethical concerns, and psychological impacts through interviews and controlled experiments.

Framework

Create 5-6 guiding principles for ethical AI use, co-designed with student input and validated through experimentation.

Impact

Promote digital literacy, reduce AI dependence, and support academic integrity in the age of artificial intelligence.
